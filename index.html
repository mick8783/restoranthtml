FAQ

How do I send photos of my bills?
Simply use our online form below to upload clear photos of both the front and back of your currency. We accept JPG and PNG formats.
What types of notes do you buy?
We buy all U.S. paper money including fancy serial numbers, rare notes, collections, original packs, and collectible currency from Colonial to Modern times.
How much can I get for my note?
The value depends on rarity, condition, and demand. We offer fair market value and provide free appraisals within 24 hours of receiving your photos.
Can I sell entire collections?
Absolutely! We specialize in buying entire collections, hoards, and estate currency. Large collections often receive premium pricing.
Is it safe to ship my notes?
Yes — we provide insured shipping labels for your protection. If you're local, we can also arrange an in-person meeting.
